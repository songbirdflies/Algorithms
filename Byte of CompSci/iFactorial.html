iFactorial

Write a function iFactorial that behaves like the following:

iFactorial(1) = 1                  => 1
iFactorial(2) = 2 * 1              => 2
iFactorial(3) = 3 * 2 * 1          => 6
iFactorial(4) = 4 * 3 * 2 * 1      => 24
iFactorial(5) = 5 * 4 * 3 * 2 * 1  => 120

Make sure your solution is iterative and not recursive.
